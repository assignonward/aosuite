Multichain Attack

Say that Alice wants to do a multichain exchange to trade some of her CCC for BCG. Charlie is advertising that he will exchange 12 CCC for 1 BCG, Alice knows Charlie and trusts him. However, Mr. X is advertising that he will exchange 10 CCC for 1 BCG, should Alice and Bob do the deal with Mr. X? How can this go wrong?
